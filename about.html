About Olls Creative Studio

Olls Creative Studio is a small digital studio focused on designing and building modern websites and mobile apps. The goal is simple: create digital products that look clean, work smoothly, and solve real problems.

Every project starts with an idea and is shaped through thoughtful design, clear structure, and careful development. I focus on building things properly — not overcomplicated, not rushed, and always with the end user in mind.

Who’s Behind the Studio

Hi, I’m Ollie — the designer and developer behind Olls Creative Studio. I build websites and apps from the ground up, handling everything from layout and UI design to development and deployment.

I’m currently working on SmartStudy, an AI-powered study app designed to help GCSE students revise smarter and track their progress. Building my own product allows me to apply the same standards, structure, and attention to detail that I bring to client work.
