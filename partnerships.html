Blue Light RolePlay Community

Official Partner • Live Now

Blue Light RolePlay Community is a Scotland-based Roblox roleplay organisation delivering high-quality, immersive emergency services roleplay with a clear focus on realism, professionalism, and structured operations. The community is inspired by real-world British policing, medical response, and rescue services — designed for members who value disciplined teamwork, clear communication, and authentic scene management.

From routine patrols and medical callouts to complex, multi-agency incidents, Blue Light RPC provides a serious, well-managed environment where standards are upheld and progression is earned through performance, reliability, and commitment to the roleplay.

Core Departments & Operational Capability

• Police Services — proactive patrol, traffic operations, incident response, prisoner handling, and structured command-led policing
• Ambulance / Medical Services — emergency medical response, patient assessment, scene coordination, triage principles, and realistic radio communications
• Specialist Operational Units — including firearms and tactical capabilities, specialist deployment, and advanced incident handling under command direction
• Structured Command & Standards — clear procedures, professional conduct expectations, and organised operations across all departments
• Live Recruitment — multiple positions open now, with active progression routes and direct entry opportunities

Recruitment is currently live across multiple departments. Blue Light RPC uses a structured, command-led process to maintain quality and professionalism. If you are applying, follow the steps below exactly to ensure your request is handled correctly.

Application Process (Required Steps)

1) Join the official Discord server using the button below.
2) Open a ticket in the main server.
3) In the ticket, ping @camo.death.
4) Send the following message exactly:

“I have come from the website i would like to apply to a position, may i see the options please ”

After submitting your ticket request, you must await a command response. Command staff will review your request and move you forward through the correct pathway. Please do not repeatedly ping command — you will be progressed when appropriate.
