Partnerships & Collaborations

I’m open to working with individuals, organisations, and brands on meaningful digital projects. Partnerships are considered where there is a clear fit in values, quality, and long-term vision.

Types of Partnerships

Product Collaboration

Collaboration on websites, apps, or digital platforms where design and development expertise is required.

Educational & EdTech Projects

Partnerships related to education, learning tools, or student-focused digital products.

Sponsorship & Support

What I Look For

  • A clear purpose or goal for the collaboration
  • Alignment with digital, technical, or educational work
  • Professional communication and realistic expectations
  • Mutual value for both sides
